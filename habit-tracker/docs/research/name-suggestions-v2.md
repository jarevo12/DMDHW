# App Name Ideas (v2)

Short, memorable, and meaning-forward names with a clear feel for a habit practice.

## Suggestions
- **Keel** — the stabilizing spine of a ship; your daily habits that keep life steady.
- **Forge** — habits as crafted strength; you build durability through repetition.
- **Riven** — a bold, sharp word; implies carving a new path by daily action.
- **Tide** — suggests rhythm and inevitability; habits that rise and return every day.
- **Atlas** — bearing the weight with structure; a guide for steady progress.
- **Bastion** — a stronghold of consistency; routines as your personal defense.
- **Vow** — simple, human, and committed; habits as small promises kept.
- **Vanta** — evokes depth and focus; a quiet, powerful place to improve.
- **Arc** — the shape of a story; habits that bend your trajectory upward.
- **Sage** — calm, deliberate growth; wiser choices through consistency.
- **Grit** — minimal and strong; progress earned through steady effort.
- **Prime** — first and essential; your core set of daily actions.

## Two-Word Blends
- **PulseTrack** — a steady beat you can follow; habits as a living rhythm.
- **TideRise** — daily lift and return; progress that keeps coming back.
- **KeelLine** — stable baseline; your habits define the line you steer by.
- **ArcCraft** — shaping your trajectory; craft your growth with repetition.
- **ForgePath** — deliberate, built progress; a path made stronger each day.
- **VowPulse** — commitment in motion; promises that keep moving forward.
- **GritFlow** — discipline plus ease; consistent effort that feels fluid.
- **PrimeAnchor** — core routines that hold everything in place.
- **SageSteps** — wise, simple actions; progress without noise.
- **AtlasPeak** — guided ascent; a clear climb toward your best.

## Axiom/Stark Blends
- **AxiomPulse** — a guiding truth in motion; daily habits that keep you aligned.
- **AxiomForge** — Principles turned into practice; routines that turn intent into identity.
- **AxiomPath** — a clear rule of the road; consistency with direction.
- **StarkFocus** — sharp, uncluttered intent; habits that cut through noise.
- **StarkRhythm** — clean, repeatable cadence; clarity through repetition.
- **StarkAnchor** — minimal and steady; routines that keep you grounded.
