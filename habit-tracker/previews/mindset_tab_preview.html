Proposed Reflection Questions

Based on the quote themes (personal responsibility, vision, process, overcoming fear, identity), these 2-3 questions help users internalize the message and commit to action:

Question 1: Reflection

"What does this quote mean for you today?"

Purpose: Helps users personalize the message and connect it to their current situation.

Question 2: Action

"What will you do differently after reading this?"

Purpose: Transforms insight into commitment. Creates accountability.

Rating System (1-5 Stars)

How much does this resonate with you?

Purpose: Enables filtering favorites later. Also provides feedback on which quotes resonate most.

Alternative Questions Considered:

  • - "What's been holding you back from embodying this mindset?"
  • - "Where in your life can you apply this wisdom today?"
  • - "What would change if you truly believed this?"
